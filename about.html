Hi! I’m Tim. I’m a freelance composer and sound designer based out of Toronto, Canada. I create music for a wide range of media (and for fun!) with a focus on video games.

I’m fascinated with the way that music and sound can enhance story-telling. I always approach composing from the perspective of creating a sense of connection and feeling for the listener.

Growing up an avid gamer, the way I write – and enjoy – music has largely been shaped by games like Chrono Trigger, Chrono Cross, Mega Man, Sonic, Zelda and Harvest Moon64

Composing for games offers the exciting chance to create something that people won’t just hear, but interact with. My goal is to create not just music, but an experience.
