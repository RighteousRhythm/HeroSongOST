Services

Music Composition

Tim has been writing music for well over a decade and is now dedicated to taking that talent and aiming it at creating engaging memorable moments. He will work hard to create the tone and feel you need to accent and solidify the world you are creating. Capable of working in a variety of styles Tim will use his creativity to give your players/viewers a unique experience.

 

Sound Design

Choosing the right sounds are a key part of establishing your world. Choosing the right door sound can mean the difference between a lovable friend busting through the door and an ominous creak signalling impending doom. It's important to know how slight changes in tone, duration and style can fundamentally change the entire vibe of a scene. Having been steeped in this kind of nuance from a young age allows Tim to confidently make those decisions and get the right feel for your world.

 

Voice

Voice work can require a lot of time and editing. Tim has years of education experience recording and editing voice. Whether you're a Podcast creator or you need voice-over work done for your game, Tim has the knowledge and skill to make sure your finished voice product sounds great!
